Avengers



The Avengers is a superhero team formed by Nick Fury, director of S.H.I.E.L.D., in 2012 to stop Loki from subjugating Earth. The original team consisted of Iron Man, Captain America, the Hulk, Thor, Black Widow, and Hawkeye. Nick Fury


Nick Fury is the director of S.H.I.E.L.D., a global peacekeeping agency, in the 2012 film The Avengers. He is a brilliant tactician and strategist, and he is responsible for assembling the Avengers team to stop Loki from subjugating Earth.
